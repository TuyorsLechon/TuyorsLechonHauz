Our Story

Family Recipe Since 1990

Tuyor's Lechon hauz has been serving perfectly roasted pigs using a secret family recipe passed down through generations. What started as a small backyard operation has grown into one of the most sought-after lechon providers in the region.

We carefully select premium native pigs and roast them slowly over charcoal for 6-8 hours to achieve that perfect crispy skin and tender, flavorful meat that our customers keep coming back for.

Our Services

Whole Roasted Pig Service

Nothing brings people together like a whole roasted lechon on the table — crispy skin, tender meat, and that rich, smoky flavor that hits just right.

We offer whole lechon packages perfect for birthdays, fiestas, reunions, corporate events, and any celebration that deserves something special. Each lechon is carefully prepared using a family recipe and slow-roasted over charcoal to ensure that golden-brown skin and juicy, flavorful meat from end to end.

  • Available sizes: From small to jumbo(kilo-based options)
  • Freshly made: No reheats, no shortcuts — roasted on the day of your event
  • Pick-up or delivery options available (depending on location)

Message us directly for pricing, size recommendations, or to reserve a date. We recommend booking ahead, especially during weekends and holidays.

We roast it like how it's meant to be — no fancy gimmicks, just honest, flavorful lechon done right.

Pa-Lechon Service

You Bring the Pig, We Bring the Flavor.

Planning a special occasion and already have a live pig? Let us do the roasting for you.

We offer Pa-Lechon services where you provide the live pig, and we handle everything else — from cleaning to roasting, done the traditional way over charcoal for that signature crispy skin and juicy meat.

Rates vary depending on the pig's weight (kilos) — just let us know how big the pig is, and we’ll give you the exact price for the roasting service. Message us for inquiries or bookings. Send us the details (pig weight and date needed), and we’ll take care of the rest.

No stress. No guesswork. Just real, delicious lechon — roasted to perfection

Paluto (Add-On) Service

Ordering a whole lechon? Don’t let the laman loob go to waste — turn it into an extra dish your guests will enjoy.

Our Paluto Add-On Service is available only for lechon customers.

We’ll take care of cooking the pig’s innards (bituka, atay, baga, etc.) into traditional, flavorful dishes you can serve alongside your lechon

You can choose from:

  • Dinuguan – Slow-cooked in vinegar and fresh pig’s blood, full of garlicky, savory richness.)
  • Halang-Halang – Spicy and gingery, perfect for those who love bold flavors.
  • Pakla – A tangy, slightly sweet sautéed mix of innards, ginger, and peppers — a Visayan classic.

Each dish is cooked fresh, lutong bahay style — no shortcuts, just honest home-style cooking

Rates are charged per 1 laman loob. Just let us know your preferred dish, and we’ll handle the rest.

Paluto is only available as an add-on when you order a Whole Roasted PiG. Send us a message for details or to include this in your booking

Price starts @ ₱300
