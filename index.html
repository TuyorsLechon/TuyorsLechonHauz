Our Story

Family Recipe Since 1990

Tuyor's Lechon hauz has been serving perfectly roasted pigs using a secret family recipe passed down through generations. What started as a small backyard operation has grown into one of the most sought-after lechon providers in the region.

We carefully select premium native pigs and roast them slowly over charcoal for 6-8 hours to achieve that perfect crispy skin and tender, flavorful meat that our customers keep coming back for.
